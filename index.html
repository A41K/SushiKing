📢 Welcome to Cooking Master! (PLEASE READ THIS)

This is a Pixel restaurant simulator where you must run your own international kitchen! Learn recipes, prepare dishes step by step, and serve customers before time runs out.

⏳ Why is this here?

This was added as a precaution because I saw a lot of players didn't understand how this worked so I made this small disclaimer to explain everything in detail.

🎯 Objective

Survive as many days as possible! Each day you’ll need to earn enough money to pay your daily expenses.

⚙️ How to Play

  • 📦 Buy ingredients from the Shop (SHOP button).
  • 🖱️ Click inventory items to spawn on canvas, drag them to stations.
  • 🍽️ Hover over items to see their recipes and how to make them
  • 📋 Match customer orders for bonus money!
  • ⏰ Keep an eye on the daily timer and order deadlines.
  • 💸 Pay your daily expenses before the day ends!
  • 🙌 Most importantly Have Fun

🌍 Unlocking Recipes

As you progress through days, you’ll unlock new cuisines — Japanese, Italian, Mexican, Indian, French and more!

📚 TUTORIAL

💡 GAME OBJECTIVE

Survive as many days as possible! Each day costs $115 in expenses. Make enough money each day to stay in business!

📅 DAY SYSTEM

• Each day lasts 10 minutes
• Daily expenses: Rent ($50), Electricity ($15), Gas ($10), Water ($8), Insurance ($12), Supplies ($20)
• You must have enough money at day's end to pay expenses
• If you can't pay, it's game over!

🔄 PROCESSING CHAIN

RAW → WASH → PEEL → CHOP → COOK → COMBINE

🥒 EXAMPLE: CUCUMBER ROLL

1. Buy cucumber from shop
2. WASH station: Clean the cucumber
3. PEEL station: Remove skin
4. CHOP station: Cut into pieces
5. Buy rice and COOK it
6. PREP station: Combine with nori
7. SERVE to customers

🎮 CONTROLS

• Click inventory items to spawn them
• Drag items to processing stations
• Drop items in top area to return to inventory
• Each station has cooldown timers
• Hover over items and orders for details
• Match customer orders for bonus money
